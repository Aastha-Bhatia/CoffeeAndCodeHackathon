General Info.

Bigg Boss is a reality television show where a group of contestants, known as housemates, live together in a specially constructed house under constant surveillance. They are isolated from the outside world and are required to complete tasks and challenges while being monitored 24/7.

The host of BiggBoss is Salman Khan. The show is presented as a reality TV program, but it is often criticized for being scripted or manipulated to some extent. While the general premise is real, some tasks, conflicts, and twists may be planned by the show's producers for entertainment value.
